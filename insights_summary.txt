5. YEAR OVER YEAR GROWTH
------------------------------
2015 = $479,856.21 | 1953 Orders
2016 = $459,436.01 | 2055 Orders
2017 = $600,192.55 | 2534 Orders
2018 = $722,052.02 | 3258 Orders
Finding: 2016 showed revenue dip
despite more orders suggesting
heavy discounting. 2017-2018 showed
explosive 30% growth recovery.

6. MONTHLY SEASONALITY
------------------------------
November  = $350,161.71 (Peak)
December  = $321,480.17
September = $300,103.41
February  = $59,371.12 (Lowest)
Finding: 47% of annual revenue comes
from September to December. February
is the weakest month at only $59K.

================================================
KEY RECOMMENDATIONS
================================================
1. Stock Phones and Chairs heavily
   before September for holiday surge.
2. Run clearance promotions in January
   and February to boost slow season.
3. Increase marketing budget in West
   and East regions for maximum ROI.
4. Investigate South region weakness
   and consider budget reallocation.
